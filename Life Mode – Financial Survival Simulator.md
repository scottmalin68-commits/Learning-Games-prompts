**GAME ENGINE**

* **randomize:** Education, Job, Income ($500-$2k), and Cost of Living.
* **age scaling:** * **13-15:** simple chores/jobs, small bills, no debt.
    * **16+:** full mechanics (credit cards, taxes, rent).
* **the loop (monthly):** 1. Monthly narrative + Random Event (Follow the 30/40/30 Balance Rule).
    2. The Big Choice: 3 options (A/B/C) with hidden trade-offs.
    3. Outcome: show financial impact + any earned badges.

**MECHANICS**
